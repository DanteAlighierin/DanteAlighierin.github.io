About

2021-02-12 — 1 min read

Hello. I am Dante and I am an IT lover. I have been working with Python for about 4 years, and also with Golang for almost a year. I use the above languages to write application software and web services. To write web services, you also need to be able to write a frontend, respectively, I have the skills and knowledge in html, css and javascript. In addition to vanilla JS, I used a VueJS.

Among other things, I am a Linux user with more than 3 years of experience. I use Void Linux as a distribution, and swayWM as a window manager. Yes, I love the KISS principle, and I use minimalistic software: sway, Void with musl. I hate systemD. Naturally I know bash well.

This blog is not aimed at commerce, here I just share my thoughts, advice and my best practices. Also, this site helps me develop writing skills and socialize.
